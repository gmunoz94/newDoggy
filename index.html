About Mobile Grooming


What is mobile grooming?

It is professional grooming self-contained in a custom van that arrives at your door to take care of your pup's grooming needs. Mobile dog grooming is preferred by most of today's busy pet owners. The convenience for you and the exclusive attention for your pet make it desirable to use our mobile dog grooming service. The inverter furnishes electricity to the dryer, table, clippers, A/C, vacuum, lights and more. A hot water heater ensures the perfect bath temperature and a therapeutic bathing system cleans and soothes your pet.


But isn't mobile grooming expensive?

You will pay more for mobile grooming. But the benefits far outweigh the additional cost.


Why should I choose mobile grooming?

  • Mobile grooming is convenient.
  • We come right to your doorstep.
  • No car sickness.
  • You save Time and Gas.
  • NO soggy doggies in your car!

Can I be in the truck with you when you groom my dog?

In our experience, this is usually not a good idea. You are your dog's favorite person, so when you are present during grooming, your dog will be focused on you and not on sitting still for or cooperating with the service. There may be some occasions where the dog's owner will be called upon to help calm or hold the dog during certain parts of the service, but generally the grooming should be a one-on-one experience between the dog and the groomer.


What is the price?

All telephone/text quotes are estimates and can only be confirmed after initial examination of your dog's condition and the time allotment required to complete the services. Prices will vary depending on size, condition of coat, temperament of dog, and haircut.
